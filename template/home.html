Welcome to the home page of our Disease_Prediction_app.

This is mainly based on as the application of the machine learning,meant to be employed in the remote area and for the people in need.

Overview of our Project

In todays time we see a lot of the shortage of the doctors in the world especially in India.A lot of people are suffering a lot without the help of the proper medical checkup.Also due to lack of timely medical checkup in most of the cases many cases arise leading to death

So to cope up with all of those problems this app is designed which would prove its benefits upto much extent.

Application

  • To remove the dependencies on the doctors
  • To help out the poor and helpless people with the normal medical checkup
  • To help people avoid paying huge amount to the doctors
  • To extend the role of the technology in the medical field
